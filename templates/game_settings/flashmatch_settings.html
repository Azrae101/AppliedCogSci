Description

Welcome to Flashmatch - The Memory Matching Game!

Test your memory and knowledge by matching terms with their definitions.

Choose your deck, difficulty level, and modes to customize your gameplay experience.

In this game, you'll need to match terms like "Photosynthesis" with their corresponding definitions. Match all the pairs to win!

Instructions

Here's how the game works:

  • Choose your deck and difficulty level.
  • Click on the cards to reveal their contents (either a term or an answer).
  • Try to match each term with its correct definition.
  • If two cards match, they'll stay revealed. If not, they'll flip back over.

Tips and advice:

  • Start with an easier difficulty to practice, then increase the challenge.
  • Use the "Endless Mode" for an unlimited number of matches, or "Time Limit" for a race against the clock!
  • Don't worry if you make a mistake; keep practicing to improve your memory!
