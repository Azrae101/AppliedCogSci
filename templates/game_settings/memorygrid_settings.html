Description

Memory Grid is a matching game where you must pair terms with their correct definitions. Test your memory and concentration as you progress through increasingly challenging levels!

Perfect for users who want to improve their recall and cognitive flexibility.

Instructions

Here's how it works:

  • Click on cards to flip them and reveal their content.
  • Match each term with its correct definition.
  • Matched pairs remain visible but grayed out.
  • Try to complete all matches with as few flips as possible!
  • Advance to higher levels for more challenge.
